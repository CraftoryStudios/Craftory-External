Overview

This powered tool is used to quickly break single blocks like a Pick-axe. Each drill is faster than their pick-axe equivalent and they do not break as they use energy rather than durability.

Tiers

Tier Capacity Mining Speed (Relative to hand) Attack Damage
Iron 30000 Re 8.4x 3 Hearts
Gold 10000 Re 16.8x 2Hearts
Diamond 100000 Re 11.2x 4 Hearts
Netherite 200000 Re 12.6x 5 Hearts

Usage

The drill is used the same as pickaxes. It is most effective on blocks like stone and ores.

Before use the drill must be charged. To charge place it in the charging slot in any cell, the higher tier cells charge faster.

100 energy is consumed for each block broken

Crafting

Iron Drill


Ingredients: Iron Block, Copper Ingot, Iron Pickaxe, Magnetised Iron, Iron Cell

Golden Drill


Ingredients: Gold Block, Copper Ingot, Golden Pickaxe, Magnetised Iron, Gold Cell

Diamond Drill


Ingredients: Diamond Block, Copper Ingot, Diamond Pickaxe, Magnetised Iron, Diamond-Cell

Netherite Drill


Ingredients: Netherite Block, Copper Ingot, Netherite Pickaxe, Magnetised Iron, Emerald-Cell
