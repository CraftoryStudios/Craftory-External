Overview

This tool can be used to destory large areas of annoy Tall Grass, or harvest crops, plants and flowers.

Left click will use the tool and break plants on the same level in a radius based on the tools level.

Usage

Left Click a plant, flower, crow or tall grass to break. It will break all plants on the same y level in a radius around.
