Copper Ore

By Brett SaundersUpdated Version 0.2.5

Overview

This block naturally generates around the world, it can be smelted into Copper Ingots or Macerated into Copper Dust.

Spawning

Copper ore will spawn at about the same rates as Iron Ore. It will only spawn in the overworld in newly generated chunks since Craftory was added to the server.
